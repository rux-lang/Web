# Support Rux

Rux is free, open source, and developed independently. You can support it with a donation, with code and documentation, or simply by telling other developers about it.

## Donate

Donations fund the time that goes into the compiler, the standard library, the tooling, and the documentation. Pick the platform that suits you best — every contribution helps.
